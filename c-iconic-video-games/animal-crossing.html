ANIMAL CROSSING

Released in the early 2000s, Animal Crossing became known for its slow pace, relaxing gameplay, and real-time world. Instead of action, it focuses on routine, community, and building a peaceful life with charming animal villagers.

WHY IT'S ICONIC

Animal Crossing is iconic because it offers a completely different kind of gaming experience—one built around comfort, routine, and personal expression. Its real-time world and soft, evolving soundtrack create a strong emotional connection, making the game feel less like something you play and more like a place you return to.
