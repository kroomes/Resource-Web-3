SUPER MARIO BROS.

Released in 1985 for the Nintendo Entertainment System, Super Mario Bros. became one of the most influential video games ever made. Its bright visuals, side-scrolling gameplay, and iconic soundtrack helped define what a platform game could be.

WHY IT'S ICONIC

Super Mario Bros. is iconic because it helped define modern video game design while introducing one of the most recognizable soundtracks ever created. Its smooth side-scrolling gameplay and clear progression became a blueprint for future games, while its music, especially the "Underground Theme”—is instantly recognizable and deeply connected to player movement and rhythm.
