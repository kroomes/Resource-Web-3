ABOUT THE ARCHIVE

This project is a digital archive documenting the history and impact of video game music and sound design.

The archive explores iconic soundtracks, influential composers, and the evolution of game audio from early 8-bit systems to modern cinematic scores.

Rather than treating game music as background detail, this archive looks at how sound helps shape atmosphere, player memory, and the emotional identity of a game.

The site is designed as a resource where users can browse different entries, compare examples, and better understand the role music plays in the video game experience.
